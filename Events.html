Join us on of our popular chef's nights!

Hosted by our very own Chef Piker

Join us for a fun filled evening with our very own Chef, Hasan Piker. Chef Piker will carefully craft a 5 course meal. Each course is paired with a wine tailored to bring out a more complex flavor with each dish it is paired with. Each evening will have an appetizer course, two entree courses, a palate cleanser course, and a dessert paired with an aperitif.

Seats are sold at $150 per person. Inquire below for tickets.
